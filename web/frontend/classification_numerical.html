CardioVascular Imaging (CVI)

Cardiovascular disease is a class of diseases that involve the heart or blood vessels. Cardiovascular disease includes coronary artery diseases (CAD) such as angina and myocardial infarction (commonly known as a heart attack). This dataset contains CardioVascular Imaging (CVI) information, which are represented as numerical values – not normalized.

In order to request a classification, select the attributes and also specify the cells for each value. For instance, for attribute Patient Age (minimum and maximum age in the dataset are 18 and 83 correspondingly), requesting 3 cells will approximately split the dataset in the following cells [18, 40), [40, 62) and [62, 83].
