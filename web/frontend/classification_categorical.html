Medical Subject Headings (MeSH)

MeSH provides a hierarchically-organized terminology for indexing and cataloging of biomedical information such as MEDLINE/PUBmed and other NLM databases. This dataset is based on a MeSH structure. In order to request a classification the attributes must be specified and should be in the form that is defined by MeSH.
